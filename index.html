PUP Student Wins Grand Prize of Asiantech Computer

Madeleine "Mhadz" Roldan of PUP came home from Baguio the proud owner of a brand new Asiantech personal computer after winning the Pinoyster Profile Bingo at the Baguio Eyeball! In town for a PUP student ad congress, Mhadz searched the audience tenaciously for 24 people whose characteristics matched those on the Bingo card given to Pinoyster members, including "has multiple body piercing", "a rapper", and "can burp for three seconds".

Pinoyster Kicks off the Eyeball Tour in Baguio

Pinoyster.com's nationwide Eyeball Tour kicked off last weekend in SM Baguio. The festivities started with a motorcade around the city with pop-alternative duo Joy and Bevs, who also performed at the Eyeball events. RGMA DJs John Blaze, Paolo, and Little Ritchie emceed the Baguio Eyeball and explained the mechanics of the Pinoyster Bring Me and Pinoyster Profile Bingo contests. For the Pinoyster Bring Me, SM Gift Cheques, Mozcom Internet Accounts and Greenwich Coupons were awarded as prizes. Joy and Bevs performed various songs and also had their own game featuring their carrier single "Kulang na Kulang" where they gave away copies of their album "Knockin' on ur Heart". The night ended with the awarding of the grand prize of a brand new Personal Computer (courtesy of Asiantech in collaboration with Red Fox and AMD) to Madeleine Roldan who successfully completed her Pinoyster Profile Bingo Card.

SM Supermalls to host Pinoyster Eyeballs

Thousands of Pinoysters will flock to SM malls across the country in the next few months, when the nationwide Pinoyster Eyeball Tour gets underway!

SM Supermalls in eight major cities around the Philippines will host the Pinoyster Eyeballs, events where members will meet each other in person and join games to win prizes. The Eyeball Tour will run from October to December 2004. Stay glued to Pinoyster.com -- Details will be released soon!
