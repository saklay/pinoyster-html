Pinoyster Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)
How do I invite people to join Pinoyster?
In the left side menu, click on Invite. The Invite Friends screen will appear. Enter your friends' e-mail addresses in the To: field, separating multiple addresses with commas (ex. user1@email.com, user2@email.net, etc.). If you wish, you may type a personal message to these friends in the Optional Personal Message field. Click Invite, and your friends will receive an e-mail invitation to join Pinoyster.
How do I add people to my Friends list?
You add users to your Friends list by requesting them to add you to their Friends list. You may request people to add you to their Friends list several ways.
The first way is to look for someone you know who may be a Pinoyster user. In the left side menu, click on User Search. The User Search window will appear. You can enter any of following criteria on which to search: e-mail address, cell phone number, or first name and last name. Click on Search to do the search. If there is a match, you may add the person to your Friends list by clicking Add <name> as Your Friend.
You may also ask people who are online to add you to their Friends list. In the Quick Stats box on the left side of the screen, click User/s Online. The User/s Online screen will appear. You may invite any of the online users to add you to their Friends list by clicking on Add <name> as Your Friend.
If you view the profile page of any user, you may request that user to add you to his/her Friends list by clicking on Add Me to Your Friends List.
In all of these cases, the user will receive an e-mail telling him/her that you have requested him/her to add you to his/her Friends list. When he/she approves that request, he/she will appear on your Friends list and you will appear on his/her Friends list.
How do I accept a connection request?
If you have a pending connection request, a connection request icon will appear in the Quick Stats box on the left side of the screen. Click on the icon and the Pending Requests screen will be displayed. Click on Yes to accept an invitation or No to decline it.
How do I upload a photo?
In the My Album section on your homepage, click on Go to My Album. The My Album screen will appear. In the Upload Photo section, enter the filename of the photo you want to upload (or click Browse and select the folder and filename of the photo you want to upload). If you wish, you may add a caption to accompany the photo. When you're done, click Submit. The photo will then appear in your album.
I was an old Pinoyster user, but when I try to log-in, I get an error message. Why can't I log-in? I've re-registered, and it seems I've lost all my old friends!
As explained in the letter we posted on the log-in screen, we parted ways with our past server on bad terms after they caused us some serious problems, and thus our previous data is as good as lost, which means that we have to start from scratch. But the cool thing is, people can now get prepaid Internet or cellphone credits for inviting people as well as chances to win weekly prizes like PlayStation 2s or Nokia cellphones. There are also some new features at the site. Click on Promo on the Navigation bar to learn the promo mechanics and see the prize for the week.
Why can't I use Chat?
Our current chatroom is a Java applet. However, not all browsers --- especially on Windows --- are configured to make use of Java. You may want to update the version of Java on your PC. You can get instructions on this at http://www.microsoft.com/mscorp/java/
In the future, we may migrate to another chatroom, but it will probably also be powered by Java.
How do I connect to mozcom using the free internet cards?
CREATING A NITRO DIALER UNDER WINDOWS 95/98
  1. On your Desktop, double-click My Computer, then Dial-Up Networking
  2. Double-click on Make New Connection
  3. Type in Nitro for the name of the computer you are dialing to
  4. Select the proper modem, click Next
  5. Enter the phone number you wish to use (see attached insert for the number of the area you are in). Disregard area code and country code for local calls. If you are dialing within a PABX system (eg. Within hotels) add 9 or the number that is used to access an outside line to the phone number to the phone number you wish to dial into. (eg. 9,2569000)
  6. Click on Next, then Finish
CREATING A NITRO DIALER UNDER WINDOWS 2000
  1. On your Desktop, double-click on My Computer, Control Panel, then Network and Dial-up Connections
  2. Double-click on Make New Connection, click Next
  3. Select the option Dial-up to the Internet, click Next
  4. Select the option I want to set up my Internet connection manually, click Next
  5. Select the option I connect through a phone line and a modem, click Next
  6. Enter the phone number you wish to use (see attached insert for the number of the area you are in)
  7. Uncheck the option Use area code and dialing rules, click Next
  8. Enter the Username and Password found at the back of your Nitro prepaid card, click Next
  9. Type in Nitro for the connection name, click Next
  10. Select the option No if the computer asks you: Do you want to set up an Internet mail account now?, click Next
  11. Mark the first box To connect to the Internet immediately, select this box and then click Finish
  12. Click Finish
CREATING A NITRO DIALER UNDER WINDOWS XP
  1. Open Internet Options in the Control Panel
  2. Click on the Connections tab
  3. Click on Add...
  4. Select Dial up to private network and click Next.
  5. Input the Mozcom number in the box and click Next.
  6. Type in Nitro for the connection name
  7. Mark the first box to connect to the Internet immediately, select this box and then click Finish.
DIAL-UP NETWORKING PROPERTIES FOR WINDOWS 95/98
  1. Right-click on the newly created dialer and click Properties
  2. Uncheck the option Use area code and Dialing Properties
  3. Click the Server Types tab and uncheck all options except for:
    • Advanced Options: Enable software compression
    • Allowed network protocols: TCP/IP
  4. Click Ok
